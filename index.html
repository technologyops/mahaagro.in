Welcome to Maha Agricultural Products Pvt Ltd.

We Produce & Export mainly Preserved Fruits & Vegetables ( Processed foods )

Since 1994 - We are in Agro Food Business

We are a Grower, Processor & Exporter of Gherkins (cornichons/baby cucumbers), Babycorn, Jalapeno Peppers, Green & Red chillies, Peppers, and other Preserved Vegetables.
Our Head Office is situated in Chennai / Tamil nadu State – South of India.
Our Factory is situated in Palacode near the borders covering major three gherkin growing states in India namely Tamilnadu, Karnataka & Andhra Pradesh. Our Farm fresh products are exported more than 56 countries worldwide.
